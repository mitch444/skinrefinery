Winnipeg Medical Aesthetics FAQs

How much does Botox cost in Winnipeg?

At Skin Refinery, neuromodulator treatments start at $9 per unit. Your total depends on your facial anatomy, goals, and dosing plan.

Do you offer natural lip filler results?

Yes. We offer mini lip filler ($300) and full lip filler ($600) with a conservative, balanced approach designed to avoid overfilled outcomes.

What is your best treatment for skin texture and acne scars?

SkinPen microneedling is one of our most-requested options in Winnipeg for texture, pores, and acne scar support, starting at $375.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book directly through our online concierge booking flow. New patient consultations are available and designed to build your personalized plan first.

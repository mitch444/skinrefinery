Ally Hohl

With over 20 years of nursing experience, Ally brings both heart and clinical expertise to her role as a medical aesthetic injector at Crestview Medical Clinic. Her background spans high-pressure specialties including emergency, oncology, organ and bone marrow transplant, and advanced wound care - so when we say youre in safe hands, we mean it!

Ally has a deep passion for helping clients look and feel their best with natural, facial balancing results. Shes known for her steady hand, gentle approach and ability to put people instantly at ease (usually with a laugh or two along the way). Whether its your first time exploring injectables or youre a seasoned aesthetic pro, Ally makes the experience both professional and personable.

Working at Crestview Medical Clinic means Ally is surrounded by a team of expert medical professionals, ensuring youre getting care thats not only beautiful-but backed by clinical excellence.

Ally completed her CNA accredited medical aesthetics training with THMA consulting.

When shes not holding a syringe, you can find Ally enjoying time with her family, in her garden or finding the perfect shade of blush!
